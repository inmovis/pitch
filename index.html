Commercial management for Real estate agencies and agents.

The problem

Phil Dunphy is a real estate agent that has his own way of working: stores all his properties in an .XLS file, writes down his customer's data in his personal agenda and doesn't realize that he's losing clients everyday because of emails never sent.

The lack of best practices makes him look unprofessional and 50% of his time is wasted.

Phil doesn't knows it yet but, if he decides to keep working like this, he will have to close his business.

Solution

A web platform that encourages the best practices for real estate agents therefore improving productivity. Inmovis helps you get listings, manage clients, turning leads into customers and get trained.

Real estate business well done.

Market size

  • 7.000 real estate agencies
  • 70% of real estate agencies are family formed without methodology or strong commercial foundations.
  • 21.000 real estate agents
  • 40 % of small real estate agents still doesn-t use any real estate software

Revenue model

  • A freemium service
  • A professional package with special benefits such as training and auto prospecting marketing leads

Growth strategy

Real estate agents are now looking to create their own private MLS, they dont want to share their listings with everyone but just with the ones they trust. Joining Inmovis allows this. Invite your fellow agent, create you private MLS.
