Commercial management for Real estate agencies and agents.

The problem

Phil Dunphy is a real estate agent that has his own way of working: stores all his properties in an .XLS file, writes down his customer's data in his personal agenda and doesn't realize that he's losing clients everyday because of emails never sent.

The lack of best practices makes him look unprofessional and 50% of his time is wasted.

Phil doesn't knows it yet but, if he decides to keep working like this, he will have to close his business.

Solution

A web platform with productivity as a core. Simple steps, customer oriented and all the most popular channels covered.

...And online commercial training for agents.
